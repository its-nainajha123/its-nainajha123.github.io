About Me

First-year student pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science & Data Analytics at IIT Patna. Curious developer, data enthusiast, and continuous learner.

Hello! I'm Naina Kumari, a first-year student pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science and Data Analytics at IIT Patna. My journey into technology has been driven by curiosity, continuous learning, and a desire to create meaningful digital experiences.

Coming from a background where opportunities had to be earned through persistence and self-learning, I developed a strong interest in problem-solving, data-driven thinking, and software development. Today, I am building my foundation in computer science through coursework in programming, mathematics, data analytics, and modern technologies.

Beyond academics, I actively explore real-world applications of technology through projects, internships, and independent learning. I have worked in digital marketing, data handling, and web development, gaining practical exposure to industry workflows while strengthening my analytical and technical skills.

At IIT Patna, I continue to expand my knowledge in computer science, data analytics, and emerging technologies. My academic journey has helped me develop a structured approach to learning, collaboration, and innovation.

I enjoy building websites, experimenting with new tools, understanding how technology solves real-world problems, and continuously improving my skills. My goal is to grow into a data scientist and technology professional who creates impactful solutions through data, software, and intelligent systems.

When I'm not studying or working on projects, I spend time reading spiritual literature, reflecting on personal growth, and exploring ideas that combine technology, learning, and purpose.

I believe that every project is an opportunity to learn, improve, and move one step closer toward creating meaningful impact through technology.
